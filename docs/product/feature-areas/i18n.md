## Product Intent

A buyer can use the entire storefront in French, English, or Russian — the languages the
Russian-speaking-woman-in-France audience actually uses — so the boutique feels native to her
from the first screen to the order message.

---

## In Scope

- All buyer-facing content available in French (primary), English, and Russian
- A way for the buyer to choose or switch their language
- Locale applied consistently across the shell, catalogue, product detail, editorial, and checkout
- French as the default/primary locale

## Out of Scope

- The actual product and editorial copy authoring per locale (Feature Area: CMS Products, Feature Area: Editorial)
- Adding locales beyond fr / en / ru (only these three for v0)
- Currency localization — prices are EUR for all locales (PRD Configuration Matrix)
- Region/shipping localization (PRD v0 exclusion — shipping handled offline)
- Admin/CMS interface translation (this area governs buyer-facing locale experience)
